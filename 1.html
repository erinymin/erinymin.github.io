My YNHD (Yeonhui-dong)

“My YNHD (Yeonhui-dong)” focuses on Yeonhui-dong, a quiet neighborhood in Seoul that exists between city and suburb. Located between the lively districts of Hongdae and Sinchon, Yeonhui-dong is defined not by noise or spectacle, but by its narrow alleys, low-rise homes, and small, local shops that support a slower, more grounded pace of life.

This project explores the atmosphere and emotional texture of the neighborhood through manipulated photographs and spatial studies, focusing on themes of mood, memory, and quiet transition. Rather than offering a direct representation, the work serves as a personal interpretation, a reflection on how everyday spaces shape our sense of belonging and identity.

My YNHD invites viewers to consider how subtle urban environments can hold meaning and nostalgia.
